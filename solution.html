How to combat Eutrophication and Red tides

So as we all know that Eutrophication and Red Tides are caused by pesticide and chemical runoffs into the ocean or into a lake or pond. In order to reduce the amount of chemical flow into the water. States should adopt a vermicomposting project. Vermicomposting reduces food waste being dumped into the ocean and also methane being released into the air. Vermicomposting is also a natural way of making fertilizer that don't release nitrogen. They also help in fertilizing the land. So agriculture can be boosted in the state. Which can increases revenue and more funding to the state. We can also focus on building awareness and raising funds to save these marvelous sites

What is Vermicomposting? And how to do it

Vermicomposting is a when you use food waste and Worms to make natural fertilizers. It is very helpful in reducing emmissions to the atmosphere and increasing revenue in agriculture fields by making land fertile.

What do you need: *Red Wiggler worms *Compost *soil *bucket or container

What to do now:

*first put a small layer of soil into the bucket

* Add Compost to the soil

* Add worms on top of the compost

* Add a small layer of soil onto the compost and the worms
