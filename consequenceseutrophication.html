Consequences of Eutrophication

Eutrophication causes loss of revenue. It also causes harm to marine species. They also kill of many native species that are also endangered in the states. Eutrophication in Michigan caused loss of millions of dollar in revenue as fishermens couldn't fish and trade suffered. It also causes the ph of the water to decrease and make in acidic in nature so water cannot be used to drink. Due to no sunlight reaching the lake floor, photosynthesis cannot be done so it reduces oxygen amount in water.
