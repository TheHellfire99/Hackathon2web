What are the consequences of Red Tides?

Red tides cause loss of millions of dollar is fishing and tourism. Due to Red tides a type of neurotoxin is produced by the Dinoflagellates and which in tun goes into the fishes. The toxin is called Brevetoxin and can cause severe poisoning. A lot peolpe have died due to eating locally fished fishes as they may have carried brevetoxin. This problem mostly occurs near Florida, Texas and Mexico due to heavy runoff in the area.
