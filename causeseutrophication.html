Causes for Eutrophication

Eutrophication is caused by residentail and agriculture runoff into the draingage system. It happens due to large amount of fertilizers being used in farming and modeling. These fertilizer mostly contain nitrogen, which is the main cause for algal bloom. when these chemicals reach the water they cause algae to rapidly populate. The runoff is largley caused by unsafe drainage systems and unsafe use of chemicals in crop. The algae poses serious threats to fishs and other marine life as they cannot breathe or eat food.
